# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-01-31

This app respects your privacy.

## Data collection
The app does **not** collect, store, or sell personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location.

## App data
The app may store limited data **locally on your device** (for example: app settings, learning progress, or preferences).  
This data never leaves your device unless explicitly stated.

## AI features
Some features may send **user-provided text** to third-party AI services in order to generate or evaluate content.  
This data is:
- sent only when you trigger the feature
- used solely to provide the requested functionality
- not used by us to identify you

## Notifications
The app may send local notifications for learning or reminder purposes.  
No personal data is shared for notifications.

## Third-party services
The app relies on standard Flutter and Android system services.  
No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

## Children’s privacy
This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
